Start your day right with a nutritious avocado and spinach omelette, packed with protein and vitamins to fuel your morning.

Healthy meals for those on a diet emphasize nutrient-rich foods like lean proteins, colorful vegetables, whole grains, and healthy fats. Portion control, balanced choices, and hydration are key. Prioritize grilled or baked proteins, plenty of veggies, and drink water to support weight management goals while staying satisfied and energized.
